There are several types of investments available, each with its own risks and rewards.
Some of the most common include:

Stocks

Investing in stocks means buying a share of a company. Investors can make money if the stock price increases or if the company pays dividends. However, the stock price can also go down, which means they can lose money.

Bonds

Bonds are a form of long-term investment in which money is lent to a company or government in exchange for interest and repayment of the borrowed principal over an agreed period of time. Bonds are considered a safer investment than stocks, but interest rates are generally lower.

ETFs

ETFs offer an easy way to invest in a wide variety of assets without having to buy individual stocks.

Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrencies are a digital form of currency that are used in place of traditional money. The value of cryptocurrencies varies widely and is considered a high-risk investment.
